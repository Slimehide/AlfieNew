About us

WHO WE ARE

Tavistock Sterling

Tavistock Sterling is a certified listing agent for The Initial License Offering Contract Exchange (ILOCX). We have extensive experience guiding companies through the legal and procedural requirements to list an ILO.

An ILO is a revenue-based form of raising capital, which is often cheaper than a normal equity capital raise. Buyers from 144 countries, along with institutional buyers, are able to purchase your company ILOs via ILOCX at the push of a button.

INITIAL LICENSE OFFERING

Listing your company on ILOCX enables you to make license sales, and receive a supercharged promotion. Buyers are required to market your product or service, directly contributing to the sales and success of your company.

As a core listing agent, Tavistock Stirling has a direct line to secure listings on ILOCX.
